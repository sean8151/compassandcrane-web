Our philosophy

China is extraordinary — and profoundly misunderstood. A thousand-mile high-speed rail journey. A misty valley where only farmers and cranes know the path. A private tea ceremony arranged by someone who grew up speaking the dialect.

Compass & Crane was built for travellers who want the real thing: unhurried, immersive, and entirely in English from first message to final day.

We handle everything — itinerary, logistics, language, culture — so you arrive as a guest, not a tourist.
